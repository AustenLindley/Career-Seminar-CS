Welcome to Dixie Brasilian, where the vibrant spirit of Brasil meets the southern charm of Athens, Alabama! We are thrilled to bring our passion for authentic Brasilian cuisine to the welcoming small community of Athens. At Dixie Brasilian, we pride ourselves on offering a unique dining experience that transports our guests to the lively streets of Rio de Janeiro, right here in the heart of Alabama. From savory churrasco grilled to perfection to colorful and refreshing caipirinhas, our menu showcases the rich diversity of Brasilian traditions. We invite you to join us on this gastronomic adventure, where the warmth of Brasilian hospitality meets the charm of Southern living. Come savor the flavors, share in the joy, and make lasting memories with us at Dixie Brasilian in Athens, where every meal is a celebration of culture and community.

Street Menu

Need a quick bite before work or just an afternoon snack? Come on in and check out our street food options. We have many different types that will satisfy anyone. You can try our Pão de Queijo (cheese bread), Picanha (barbecued beef), or Kibe (meat patties). If you have a sweet tooth, come and try our Pastel de Queijo (deep-fried cheese pastry) or Açai na Tigela (Açai in the Bowl).

Afternoon Menu

Our lunch menu won't disappoint you; we have a wide range of premium meats that will leave you feeling full.

Dinner Menu

Explore our highly favored authentic Churrasco options from Fraidinha, Sausages, and Garlic-Covered Picanha.
